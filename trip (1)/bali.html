About This Trip

TRIP DESCRIPTION

Enjoy the charm of the Island of Gods in a different way. We combine the cultural serenity of Ubud, the exotic island adventure of Nusa Penida, and the luxury of Uluwatu sunsets into one complete package. This trip is very flexible and perfect for a short weekend healing. Enjoy the crashing waves at Kelingking Beach and the spiritual atmosphere at Tirta Empul Temple without worrying about transportation.

ITINERARY

Day 1 : Arrival & Uluwatu Sunset

  • Pick up at Ngurah Rai Airport
  • Check-in Hotel in Kuta/Seminyak
  • Visit Uluwatu Temple
  • Watch Kecak Fire Dance at Sunset
  • Seafood Dinner at Jimbaran Bay

Day 2 : Nusa Penida Island Tour

  • Transfer to Sanur Harbor
  • Fast boat to Nusa Penida
  • Visit Kelingking Beach (T-Rex Cliff)
  • Visit Angel's Billabong & Broken Beach
  • Relax at Crystal Bay
  • Return to Bali mainland

Day 3 : Ubud Culture Tour

  • Visit Tegalalang Rice Terrace (Swing available)
  • Visit Ubud Sacred Monkey Forest
  • Visit Tirta Empul (Holy Spring Temple)
  • Coffee Plantation visit

Day 4 : Shopping & Departure

  • Visit Krishna Oleh-Oleh for souvenirs
  • Transfer to Airport
  • End of Trip
