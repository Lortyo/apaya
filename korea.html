About This Trip

TRIP DESCRIPTION

Annyeonghaseyo! Feel the energetic pulse of South Korea. This package takes you to the heart of global pop culture in Seoul and the natural serenity of Nami Island. Visit your favorite K-Drama filming locations, take photos wearing Hanbok at ancient royal palaces, and shop for the best cosmetics in Myeongdong. This trip is a perfect balance between traditional history and modern pop culture.

ITINERARY

Day 1 : Arrival in Incheon

  • Arrive at Incheon International Airport
  • Meet & Greet with local guide
  • Transfer to Seoul Hotel
  • Check-in

Day 2 : Nami Island Tour

  • Visit Nami Island (Winter Sonata Filming Location)
  • Visit Petite France & Italian Village
  • Return to Seoul

Day 3 : Seoul City Tour

  • Visit Gyeongbokgung Palace (Hanbok Experience included)
  • Visit Bukchon Hanok Village
  • Visit Insadong Antique Street

Day 4 : Shopping Day

  • Visit Ginseng & Cosmetic Center
  • Shopping at Myeongdong Street
  • Shopping at Hongdae Youth Street

Day 5 : Gangnam District

  • Visit Starfield Library (Coex Mall)
  • Gangnam K-Star Road
  • Photo stop at N Seoul Tower (Love Locks)

Day 6 : Departure

  • Visit Local Supermarket for souvenirs
  • Transfer to Incheon Airport
  • Flight back to Jakarta
