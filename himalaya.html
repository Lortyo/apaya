About This Trip

TRIP DESCRIPTION

Challenge yourself to step foot on the roof of the world. The Everest Base Camp (EBC) expedition is every adventurer's dream journey. Guided by professional and certified Sherpas, your safety is our top priority. This journey is not just a trek, but a spiritual journey witnessing the grandeur of the Himalayas, visiting ancient Buddhist monasteries in the mountains, and experiencing the hospitality of the local Sherpa people.

ITINERARY

Day 1 : Arrival in Kathmandu

  • Arrival at Tribhuvan International Airport
  • Transfer to Hotel in Thamel
  • Trip Briefing & Equipment Check

Day 2 : Flight to Lukla & Trek to Phakding

  • Scenic Flight to Lukla (2,860m)
  • Begin Trek to Phakding (2,610m) - approx. 4 hours

Day 3 : Trek to Namche Bazaar

  • Trek to Namche Bazaar (3,440m) - approx. 6 hours
  • Enter Sagarmatha National Park
  • First glimpse of Mt. Everest

Day 4 : Acclimatization Day

  • Rest day in Namche Bazaar for altitude acclimatization
  • Hike to Everest View Hotel for panoramic views

Day 5-8 : Trek to Gorakshep

  • Trek via Tengboche Monastery (3,860m)
  • Trek to Dingboche & Lobuche
  • Reach Gorakshep (5,164m)

Day 9 : Everest Base Camp & Kala Patthar

  • Hike to Everest Base Camp (5,364m)
  • Hike to Kala Patthar (5,545m) for sunrise view
  • Begin descent

Day 10-13 : Descent to Lukla

  • Trek back down via Pheriche and Namche Bazaar
  • Final celebration dinner in Lukla

Day 14 : Flight to Kathmandu & Departure

  • Fly back to Kathmandu
  • Transfer to International Airport
