About This Trip

TRIP DESCRIPTION

Explore the wonders of Japan in 7 amazing days. This journey covers the legendary "Golden Route", taking you from the futuristic neon bustle of Tokyo to the serene temples of Kyoto, and ending in the culinary paradise of Osaka. This package is ideal for first-timers visiting Japan. You will experience riding the Shinkansen (bullet train), wearing a Kimono, and enjoying authentic sushi right in its homeland.
